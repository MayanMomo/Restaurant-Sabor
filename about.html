Our Story

Our Philosophy

At Restaurante Sabor, our philosophy is simple: food is memory. We believe in honouring the past by using the authentic, bold flavours of Portugal.

We partner with local farmers, fishermen, and winemakers to ensure every dish is a celebration of the finest ingredients our region has to offer. From the sea to the table, our commitment is to freshness and quality.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Sofia Almeida

Head Chef & Founder

Born in Lisbon and trained in the finest kitchens of Europe, Chef Sofia returns to her roots with Sabor. Her vision is to deconstruct traditional dishes and rebuild them with a refined, modern elegance. Her passion is in every plate.
